KOLE ADEMULEGUN

1953 - 2023

Partner/Founder and Vice Chairman at MediaLink Ltd, Fellow of the Advertising Practitioner Council of Nigeria (APCON) now ARCON and the Former President of the Outdoor Advertising Association of Nigeria (OAAN).

Tributes

Steve Babaeko, President of the Association of Advertising Agencies of Nigeria (AAAN)

Mr. Kole Ademulegun was one of the pillars of our industry, a leading light that blazed the trail for new generations of practitioners, especially in the Out of home sector. The news of his passing is both shocking and heartbreaking. On behalf of the Executive Board of the Association of Advertising Agencies of Nigeria (AAAN), we send our deepest condolences to his family and his colleagues. Our prayers are with them at this very difficult time.

Dr. Olalekan Fadolapo, Director-General of the Advertising Regulatory Council of Nigeria (ARCON)

"A distinguished Fellow and a respected practitioner has gone home to be with the Lord. We will surely miss him. Adieu Mr. Kole Ademulegun.

Femi Adelusi, President of Media Independent Independent Association of Nigeria (MIPAN) and Chairman, Heads of Advertising Sectoral Groups (HASG)

"The news of the passing of Mr. Kole Ademulegun is a sad one. The industry is yet to recover from the shocking passing of Mr. Tolu Ogunkoya and then this news. I met Mr. Ademulegun early in my entrance into the industry and used his professional experience and passion in running a successful business. He sacrificed time and effort in driving collaboration across all sectoral groups and clients. He always demonstrated a friendly, warm, and amiable persona. He will be missed. I pray that the Lord comforts his family and friends
